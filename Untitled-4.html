About Us

RDS Architect Engineer is a premium architecture and design studio founded and led by its visionary Founder & CEO, Mr. Raj. With advanced-level expertise and a strong foundation in modern architectural practices, he has established a reputation for delivering sophisticated, high-quality design solutions since 2020.

Mr. Raj is known for his refined approach to architecture, where creativity meets precision and functionality blends seamlessly with aesthetics. His expertise spans across 2D planning, 3D elevation, and luxury interior design, creating spaces that reflect elegance, innovation, and timeless appeal.

Every project undertaken by RDS Architect Engineer is a reflection of excellence, where attention to detail, modern design trends, and vastu principles are carefully integrated to deliver outstanding results. His work is not just about constructing spaces but about crafting experiences that define lifestyle and comfort.

Under his leadership, the firm has successfully delivered a diverse range of residential and commercial projects, earning trust through professionalism, quality, and client satisfaction. His vision is to transform ordinary spaces into extraordinary landmarks that stand out with uniqueness and class.

RDS Architect Engineer is more than a design firm — it is a symbol of luxury, innovation, and architectural excellence, driven by passion and committed to creating spaces that inspire and impress.
